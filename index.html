About Hexline

Who we are

Since 2018 our tiny enthusiastic team is working to help people produce better documents. Every design is made by hand and already tested in fields. We already helped thousands people to land their dream job and we won’t stop.

A bit on templates

We’re focusing on MS Word and Figma templates since they are most userfriendly. If you don’t know Figma don’t panic! It’s simple yet powerful tool designers use to create digital stuff. We really recommend it. You can work even in your browser for free!

Great news

Hexline is mostly enthusiastic project and that’s the reason you will find most of templates are free. This will never change. To keep things running we are planning to make some paid services. But you can also support us if you want!
